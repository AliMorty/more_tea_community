About Us

Starting February 2026, we, a group of grad students, decided to start a weekly tea event on Wednesdays from 12pm to 2pm in ECS 668. The idea is to have a place where people can gather. I decided to take the initiative and now I want to invite you to the first of these events, which happens this week, February 25, 2026.

I will provide tea and cups!

If you are a Grad Student, Staff, or Faculty, or a researcher, or inspired to do research someday, you are very welcome to come by. I have made a mailing list too, just in case we need to make any announcements.

Here is the link to the Google Group:

groups.google.com/g/more-tea-events

(Please feel free to share it with other friends who might be interested to join)

Warm regards,
