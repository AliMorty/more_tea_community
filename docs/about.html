About

Who we are and why we gather.

Our Story

More Tea Community started as a simple idea: bring people together over tea. Every Wednesday at the UVIC Engineering and Computer Science building (ECS), students, faculty, and researchers gather on the 6th floor lounge for an hour or two of conversation, connection, and of course, plenty of tea.

There is no agenda, no membership fee, and no formal structure. Just a shared love of tea and the belief that good communities are built one cup at a time.

Caffeinated and decaf options are always provided. Everyone is welcome, whether it is your first time or your fiftieth.

Where We Meet

Location: ECS Building, 6th Floor Lounge, University of Victoria
When: Every Wednesday, 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
Cost: Free
