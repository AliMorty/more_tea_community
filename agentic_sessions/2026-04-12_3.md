# Session Summary - 2026-04-12 (Session 3)

## High-level Summary

Two main changes were made this session:

1. **Reversed the homepage scroll behavior** — Previously, the hero photo was shown on page load and the calendar/join section appeared after scrolling down. Now the calendar and "Join Us" links are visible immediately on load, and the hero photo is revealed by scrolling up. This makes the most useful content (calendar + links) front and center.

2. **Font updates on the About page** — Experimented with Calisto MT across several elements. The final result:
   - About page body text (`<p>` tags) switched to Calisto MT, weight reduced from 600 to 400 (normal).
   - About page heading changed from "How it first started" to "The First Invitation Message", set to Calisto MT, bold, non-italic.
   - Footer tagline switched to Calisto MT.
   - Tried Calisto MT on the photo caption, nav links, and page hero heading but reverted those — didn't look good.
