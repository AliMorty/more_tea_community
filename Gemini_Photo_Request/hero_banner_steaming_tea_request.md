# Image Request: Hero Banner - Steaming Tea

## Purpose

This image is used as the hero/banner background on the More Tea Community homepage. It sits behind the headline text "Wednesdays at UVIC ECS - Community, Connection, And plenty of tea."

## Description for Gemini

Generate a wide, warm, photorealistic image of a glass mug of dark amber tea sitting on a wooden table. The mug should have visible steam rising from it. Soft, warm lighting - like a cozy indoor cafe or study lounge. There may be a rock sugar candy stick or a lemon slice nearby as a prop. The mood should feel welcoming, calm, and community-oriented. Subtle depth of field with a blurred background.

## Specs

- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 (wide banner, landscape orientation)
- **Minimum size**: 1200 x 500 px
- **Style**: photorealistic, warm tones, cozy atmosphere
- **Colors to favor**: amber, brown, cream, warm gold
- **Colors to avoid**: cold blues, grays, anything clinical or sterile

## Where to save

Once generated, save the file as:
`docs/images/hero.png`

Then update `docs/index.html` to reference it in the hero section background.
